Pizza is yeasted flatbread generally topped with
tomato sauce and cheese and baked in an oven. It
is commonly topped with selection of meats,vegetables
and condiments.


The term was recoeded inthe 10th century, in a
Latin manuscript from Gaeta in Central Italy.


The modern pizza was invented in Naples,Italy and
the dish and its variants have since become popular
and common in many areas of the world.
